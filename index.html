Welcome to Grey Navies Foundation

A non-governmental organisation which aims at promoting education, respect for cultural diversity and supporting those in need.

About Us

"Grey Navies Foundation is a community-driven non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering lives through education, inclusion, and compassion. We believe that every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves access to opportunity and dignity. Our mission is to promote education, cultural respect, and social support through programs that uplift communities in need. From providing school materials to running mentorship programs, we work hand-in-hand with local partners to create lasting impact. We are especially committed to supporting vulnerable groups such as orphans, youth, and the elderly. By fostering unity, understanding, and self-reliance, we help communities become stronger from within. Together, we are building a future where everyone has a chance to thrive".

What We Do

We’re dedicated to uplifting the less privileged — children, the elderly, and women — through impactful programs in education, health, and livelihoods.

Supporting Children

Empowering kids through education, health programs, and safe spaces to grow and thrive.

Elderly Support

We offer healthcare checkups, home assistance, and social programs that bring joy, dignity, and connection to our senior community members.

Empowering Women

From literacy training to health outreach and economic empowerment, we stand beside women on their path to independence and leadership.

Children Programs

Our work with children includes education scholarships, school supply drives, health screenings, nutrition, and safe spaces for play and growth.
